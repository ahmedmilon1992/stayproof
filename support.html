Frequently asked

Do I need an account? No. StayProof works without sign-up; your data stays on your device.

How do I document a stay? Tap "Start New Proof", pick the type, then follow the guided room-by-room capture at check-in and again at check-out.

How do I export a report? Open the stay's report and tap View PDF, then Share or Email.

How do I manage my subscription? Manage or cancel anytime in your iPhone Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Will I lose my data? Your proof is stored on your device. Keep an iPhone backup on, or use "Save to Photos" / export PDFs to keep extra copies.
